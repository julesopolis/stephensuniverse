Key

The places of memory in Rome are separated into two main time periods: Classical Rome and Rome during the Renaissance and Baroque eras. These places of memory associated with each icon were erected either during that time period or was a main center of culture for the people of Rome during that era. Although these places continue to be areas of worldwide interest, mostly as tourist attractions, we have placed them within their periods of construction and original, intended use. This is by no means an exhaustive list but acts as a starting point for those who wish to begin exploring the history of Rome, one of the most culturally and historically complex cities in the world.
